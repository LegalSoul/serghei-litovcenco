The Return of Algiz

In this rune-themed puzzle-action game the player gets to control gravity by rotating the whole environment around. In order to bring the Protecting Algiz rune back, the Light Orb will have to go through the perils of each Runestone and cleanse them from the darkness.

I created the game from concept to implementation using Unity engine, including the soundtrack, composed in LMMS.

Elder Futhark runes lie in the core of the game's world. Each level represents one rune and reflects its meaning. The player will go through rune levels that represent Ice, Fire, Sun, Equilibrium, and many others, solving gravitational puzzles and mazes, avoiding traps, and repelling dangers.

LS3D - OpenGL 3D Viewer

My OpenGL viewer and editor that allows loading 3D models and textures and applying modifications.

The list of supported features:

  • Loading models and textures with normal mapping support
  • Scaling, translating, and rotating the model in the viewport
  • Phong Shading support

  • Laplacian smoothing
  • Polygon optimization using the edge collapse method
  • Laplacian Operator for morphing the model against anchors while retaining general original shape characteristics

  • Other functionalities, such as flat shading, wireframe, triangulation, and noise addition

Tools and Mods

I contributed to Resident Evil 4 modding community by developing mods and modding tools.
Kotaku gaming blog wrote an article about some of my work. The YouTube videos that showcase my 4 published mods have gathered over 2 million views in total.

My latest mod is a nearly global Devil May Cry 3 mod that replaces all characters and enemies across the whole RE4 game and cutscenes. I also made in-game animation and UI edits, sound replacements, writings, and inventory items replacement.

I posted the following modding tutorials and developed supporting tools for them:

Toxic Destroyer

A demo project based on arkanoid mechanics with toxic colors. The levels are dynamic and have different brick types and powerups.

The demo was created by me and used in a game development course I conducted at an IT academy.

Memories Walk

I made this game for 2 players just for fun. The tiny rabbits are running through photos of me and my wife. The objects, buildings, and some lines from the photos act like obstacles and platforms for the players, creating an amusing experience of "walking the photos".

Gears of Love

A fun minigame for 2 players where two furry balls are running around and trying to cross the bridge. The players need to solve a gear-chain puzzle to fix the bridge.

Conway's Game of Life simulation

My Unity implementation of Conway's Game of Life concept. The project was part of the Interactive Storytelling course at the Technical University of Moldova, where I worked as an univeristy assistant.

Game of Life is cellular automation with fairly simple rules based on underpopulation, survival, and overpopulation. However, behind the simplicity hides the sheer vastness of patterns the cells can evolve to.
